The Placement Portal is a comprehensive tool created to make hiring students, businesses, and institutions easier. Businesses can post job openings, students can apply for those positions, and all parties can follow the application process in real time. Recruiters and candidates may easily manage job postings, applications, and communication through the system's user-friendly interface. Additionally, it facilitates comprehensive analytics to track placement data and success rates, improving the effectiveness of campus placements.
